# Privacy Policy for TabTherapist

**Last updated:** April 19, 2026

I am committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how TabTherapist ("the Extension") handles your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
To provide its core functionality (calculating your Tab Hoarding Score), the Extension accesses the following information from your browser:
* **Web History (Tab URLs):** I read the URLs of your currently open tabs.
* **Timestamps:** I track when tabs were opened to calculate "tab age."

## 2. How I Use Information
This data is used solely to calculate your Tab Hoarding Score, identify duplicate tabs, and provide management actions within the dashboard.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
* **Local-First:** All data is processed and stored locally on your device using the `chrome.storage` API.
* **No Server Uploads:** I do NOT send your tab data, history, or any personal information to any external servers or third parties.
* **No Tracking:** I do not use any telemetry or analytics tracking.

## 4. Third-Party Disclosure
I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated date.

## 6. Contact
If you have questions, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
